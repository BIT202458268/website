Kenyan Curriculum — Quick Overview

A clear, compact summary of the national curriculum and what it means for learners today.

What it is

Kenya follows a competency-focused approach (CBC) that is evolving toward a broader Competency‑Based Education (CBE). The emphasis is on skills, values and applied knowledge rather than rote memorization.

How it's structured

  • Pre-primary: PP1–PP2
  • Primary: Grades 1–6
  • Junior Secondary: Grades 7–9
  • Senior School: Grades 10–12 (rollout underway)

Why it matters

  • Focus on competencies (critical thinking, collaboration, digital skills)
  • More project- and skills-based learning
  • Pathways in Senior School (STEM, Arts & Sports, Social Sciences)
